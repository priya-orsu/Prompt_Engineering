### **Example 1**

##### **Vague Prompt:**

###### “Explain Artificial Intelligence.”



##### **Improved Prompt:**

###### “Explain Artificial Intelligence in 5 simple bullet points suitable for a 10th-grade student. Include one real-life example.”

###### 

##### **Why This Is Better:**

###### Specifies format (5 bullet points)

###### Specifies audience level (10th grade)

###### Requests example

###### Reduces ambiguity



##### **Output Comparison:**

###### **Vague Prompt Output**	             **Improved Prompt Output**

###### Long, technical explanation	     Clear, simple, structured

###### May include advanced terms	     Easy to understand

###### No guaranteed example	             Includes practical example



##### **Effect:** 

###### The improved prompt produces clearer, more targeted, and structured output.





### **Example 2**



##### **Vague Prompt:**

###### “Tell me about Python.”



##### **Improved Prompt:**

###### “Describe the Python programming language in 6 bullet points, including its features, advantages, and two real-world applications.”



##### **Why This Is Better:**

###### Clarifies that Python = programming language

###### Requests specific points

###### Defines structure

###### Asks for applications

###### 

##### **Output Comparison:**

###### **Vague	                             Improved**

###### May talk about snake	             Clearly about programming

###### Random details	                     Organized features + uses

###### No structure	                     Structured and informative



##### **Effect:** 

###### Improved clarity prevents misunderstanding and produces relevant content.





### **Example 3**



##### **Vague Prompt:**

###### “Write about climate change.”



##### **Improved Prompt:**

###### “Explain climate change in 3 short paragraphs, covering (1) causes, (2) effects, and (3) solutions. Keep the language simple.”

###### 

##### **Why This Is Better:**

###### Defines structure (3 paragraphs)

###### Specifies topics to cover

###### Controls tone (simple language)



##### **Output Comparison:**

###### **Vague	                             Improved**

###### May focus only on causes	     Balanced explanation

###### Could be too long	             Controlled length

###### No clear structure	             Organized and complete



##### **Effect:** 

###### The improved prompt ensures completeness and readability.
